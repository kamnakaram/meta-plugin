## Andromeda & Advantage+ (2025-2026)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| 25 diverse creatives | ~17% more conversions at ~16% lower cost |
| Creative Similarity Score >60% | Triggers retrieval suppression (Andromeda penalty) |
| Advantage+ Shopping ROAS | ~22% higher ROAS |
| Advantage+ Shopping CPA | ~11.7% CPA improvement |
| Creative fatigue lifespan | Compressed to ~2-4 weeks (was 6-8 weeks) |

## Creative Fatigue Thresholds

| Signal | Warning | Fail |
|--------|---------|------|
| Frequency (prospecting, 7d) | >3 | >5 |
| Frequency (retargeting, 7d) | >8 | >12 |
| CTR decline over 14 days | — | >20% |
| Exposures before conversion drop (~45%) | 4 | — |
| Top-of-funnel creative lifespan | 3-4 weeks | — |
| Bottom-of-funnel creative lifespan | 6-7 weeks | — |

## Pixel / CAPI & EMQ

| Metric | Healthy | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| EMQ (Purchase) | ≥8.0 | ~87% of advertisers sit below 8.0 |
| Dedup rate | ≥90% | Below 70% double-counts conversions |
| Server/browser hit ratio | 80-120% | <50% means iOS/ITP loss isn't recovered |
| Signal loss without CAPI (post-iOS 14.5) | 30-40% | The reason CAPI is a hard requirement |

## Minimum Viable Meta Budget

| Situation | Minimum monthly | Rationale |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------|
| Single conversion campaign | $600-$800 | ~50 events/week/ad set × CPA to exit learning |
| Full-funnel structure | ~$3,000+ | Prospecting + retargeting + testing each need budget |
| High-CPM verticals (finance/legal) | $3,000-$5,000+ | High CPM raises the floor to gather signal |
