# Info Products & Courses Meta Advertising Template

## Industry Characteristics

- Funnel-driven: opt-in → nurture → webinar/VSL → offer
- Meta is the primary cold-traffic engine for most info-product businesses
- Creative and hook quality dominate results; the algorithm rewards distinct concepts
- Compliance-sensitive: income/results claims must be honest and policy-safe
- Low-ticket vs high-ticket funnels need different objectives and pacing

## Recommended Objective & Placement Mix

| Layer | Objective | Budget % | Why |
|-------|-----------|----------|-----|
| Core | Leads (opt-in / webinar registration) | 45-60% | Fill the funnel cheaply |
| Sales | Sales (low-ticket tripwire / course) | 15-25% | Monetize and feed the pixel purchase event |
| Retargeting | Sales / Leads (VSL, webinar replay) | 15-25% | Convert warm registrants |
| Testing | Reels-first hooks | 5-10% | Constant fresh angles |

## Campaign Architecture

```
Ad Account
├── Prospecting (Leads) — opt-in / webinar
│   ├── Ad Set: Broad, creative-led (Reels-first)
│   └── Ad Set: 1-3% Lookalike of buyers
├── Sales — low-ticket offer
├── Retargeting — registrants, VSL viewers, checkout starters
└── Testing — hooks, creators, offers
```

## Creative Strategy

- **Hook-first UGC / talking-head** — the first 1-2s decide everything
- **Story/transformation** (BAB, Star-Story-Solution) angles
- **Proof**: student results (compliant, specific, non-guaranteed)
- **Curiosity + specificity** in the hook ("the 3-step X that…")
- Rotate genuinely distinct hooks weekly — this niche fatigues fast

## Targeting Strategy

- **Broad + Advantage+ Audience**, let creative do the targeting
- **Lookalikes** of buyers (not just opt-ins)
- **Retargeting** registrants and video viewers heavily
- Exclude existing students from cold prospecting

## Budget & Benchmarks

| Metric | Info-product Meta benchmark (directional) |
|--------|-------------------------------------------|
| Meta CPM | $8-$20 |
| Cost per opt-in | $1-$8 |
| Cost per webinar registration | $3-$15 |
| Min viable monthly budget | ~$1,500-$3,000 |

### Bidding
- **Highest Volume** for opt-ins; **Cost Cap** on the purchase event once stable
- Track and import the **purchase** event (not just opt-in) so Meta optimizes to revenue

## KPI Targets

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 3 | Month 6 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Cost per opt-in | Baseline | -20% | -30% |
| Opt-in→purchase rate | Track | Improve | Improve |
| ROAS (blended/MER) | Track | 2.0+ | 3.0+ |

## Common Pitfalls

- Non-compliant income/results claims → account restriction (a real, common risk)
- Optimizing to opt-ins only; buyers scale differently
- Recycling the same hook — Andromeda clusters it and CPMs climb
- No retargeting on registrants/VSL viewers
