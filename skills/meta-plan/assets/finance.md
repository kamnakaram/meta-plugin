# Financial Services Meta Advertising Template

## Industry Characteristics

- **Credit is a Special Ad Category** — restricted targeting for credit offers
- Heavily policy-scrutinized: no misleading financial promises, clear disclosures required
- Success = qualified applications/leads, then funded accounts/policies
- High LTV supports higher CPL, but fraud and lead quality are constant risks
- Some products (loans, crypto, insurance) have extra restrictions or need certification

## Compliance (do this first)

- Declare **Credit** as the Special Ad Category for credit products (no age/gender/ZIP,
  broadened radius, no Lookalikes → use Special Ad Audiences)
- **No unrealistic/get-rich-quick claims**; include required disclosures (APR, terms)
- Check product-specific rules (loans, crypto, insurance, investments) and any
  certification/licensing requirements before launch

## Recommended Objective & Placement Mix

| Layer | Objective | Budget % | Why |
|-------|-----------|----------|-----|
| Core | Leads (application/quote Instant Forms) | 50-65% | Capture qualified intent |
| Retargeting | Leads / Sales to applicants | 20-30% | Convert warm, comparison-shopping users |
| Awareness | Video views (education, trust) | 10-20% | Build credibility |

## Campaign Architecture

```
Ad Account (Credit Special Ad Category where applicable)
├── Prospecting (Leads) — compliant targeting
│   ├── Ad Set: Offer-led (rate/quote/approval odds)
│   └── Ad Set: Special Ad Audience (for credit)
├── Retargeting — applicants, quote-starters
└── Awareness — education / trust building
```

## Creative Strategy

- **Clear, honest offer** (rate, savings, "check without affecting score")
- **Trust signals**: licensing, security, ratings, real disclosures
- **Educational** angles (how to X) that pre-qualify
- **Testimonials** with realistic, compliant outcomes
- Avoid hype, guarantees, and prohibited financial claims

## Targeting Strategy

- Compliant targeting (Special Ad Category rules for credit)
- **Custom Audiences** from consented CRM lists; **Special Ad Audiences** for credit
- Retargeting of quote/application starters; strong fraud-aware lead qualification

### Bidding
- **Highest Volume** for volume; **Cost Cap** once cost-per-funded is stable
- Import the **funded/approved** event via CAPI so Meta optimizes past the form fill

## KPI Targets

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 3 | Month 6 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Cost per funded/approved | Baseline | -15% | -25% |
| Lead→funded rate | Track | Improve | Improve |

## Common Pitfalls

- Not declaring the Credit Special Ad Category (policy violation)
- Non-compliant claims/disclosures → account restriction
- Optimizing to raw leads with rampant fraud/low fund rates
- No downstream (funded) event imported for optimization
