# Real Estate Meta Advertising Template

## Industry Characteristics

- **Housing is a Special Ad Category** on Meta — targeting is restricted by law/policy
- No age, gender, ZIP, or detailed-targeting narrowing; a broad, minimum-15-mile radius applies; no Lookalikes
- Success = qualified buyer/seller/renter leads, then showings and closings
- Long consideration; retargeting and listing catalogs do heavy lifting
- Highly local and visual — video tours perform

## Special Ad Category Compliance (do this first)

- Declare **Housing** as the Special Ad Category on every housing campaign
- Expect restricted targeting: no age/gender, no ZIP-level, broadened radius, no
  Lookalikes, limited detailed targeting
- Copy/creative must avoid discriminatory language or implications
- Use **Special Ad Audiences** (compliant analog) instead of standard Lookalikes where offered

## Recommended Objective & Placement Mix

| Layer | Objective | Budget % | Why |
|-------|-----------|----------|-----|
| Core | Leads (Instant Forms) | 50-65% | Capture buyer/seller/renter interest |
| Retargeting | Traffic/Leads to listings | 20-30% | Re-touch engaged prospects (within category rules) |
| Awareness | Video views (tours, agent brand) | 10-20% | Build local recognition |

## Campaign Architecture

```
Ad Account (Housing Special Ad Category declared)
├── Prospecting (Leads) — broad radius, compliant targeting
│   ├── Ad Set: Buyer intent (listing offer / home valuation)
│   └── Ad Set: Seller intent ("what's your home worth")
├── Retargeting — listing viewers, form starters
└── Awareness — video tours / agent brand
```

## Creative Strategy

- **Video walkthroughs / neighborhood tours** (9:16, sound-on)
- **"What's your home worth"** seller lead magnets
- **Agent-on-camera** trust and local expertise
- **Listing carousels** for active inventory
- Keep copy compliant — sell the property/service, never the audience

## Targeting Strategy

- Compliant broad radius (no ZIP narrowing); let creative + offer qualify
- **Special Ad Audiences** where available (not standard Lookalikes)
- Retargeting of on-site listing viewers within category rules

## Budget & Benchmarks

| Metric | Real-estate Meta benchmark (directional) |
|--------|------------------------------------------|
| Meta CPM | $9-$20 |
| Cost per lead | $5-$40 (buyer) / higher for seller |
| Min viable monthly budget | ~$1,500-$3,000 |

### Bidding
- **Highest Volume** for lead volume; qualify with Instant Form questions
- Import downstream (appointment/showing) events via CAPI where possible

## Common Pitfalls

- Forgetting to declare the Housing Special Ad Category (policy violation / disapprovals)
- Using restricted targeting (ZIP/age) → rejections
- Discriminatory language in copy/creative
- No retargeting on listing viewers
