# Local Service Meta Advertising Template

## Industry Characteristics

- Geographically constrained demand; radius targeting is essential
- Speed-to-lead matters — leads must reach the business fast
- Success = booked calls, form leads, or messages, then jobs
- Smaller budgets; efficiency and audience size limits are real constraints
- Reviews and trust signals drive conversion heavily

## Recommended Objective & Placement Mix

| Layer | Objective | Budget % | Why |
|-------|-----------|----------|-----|
| Core | Leads (Instant Forms) or Calls | 55-70% | Fastest path to booked jobs on mobile |
| Messaging | Click-to-Messenger / WhatsApp | 15-25% | Low-friction inbound for service questions |
| Retargeting | Leads to site / reviews | 10-15% | Re-touch people who didn't submit |
| Testing | Reels awareness (local) | 5-10% | Cheap local reach |

## Campaign Architecture

```
Ad Account
├── Prospecting (Leads / Calls) — radius around service area
│   ├── Ad Set: Instant Form (name, phone, job type)
│   └── Ad Set: Click-to-call / Messenger
├── Retargeting (Leads)
│   └── Ad Set: Site visitors + form starters (14-30 days)
└── Testing
    └── Offers (free quote, seasonal tune-up), creative angles
```

## Creative Strategy

- **Before/after** job results (the strongest local proof)
- **Owner / crew on camera** (UGC trust) explaining the service and guarantee
- **Review spotlights** with star ratings and neighborhood names
- **Offer-led**: free quote, seasonal special, financing available
- **Fast, mobile-first 9:16** for Reels/Stories

## Targeting Strategy

- **Radius / ZIP** targeting around the service area (mind audience-size floors)
- **Broad** within the radius — small local audiences don't need narrow interests
- **Custom Audiences** from past-customer lists for win-back and Lookalikes
- Watch **frequency** — small audiences saturate fast; refresh creative early

## Budget & Benchmarks

| Metric | Local service Meta benchmark (directional) |
|--------|--------------------------------------------|
| Meta CPM | $7-$16 |
| Cost per lead | $8-$50 depending on trade/ticket |
| Frequency (7d) | keep <4 on small radii |
| Min viable monthly budget | ~$1,000-$2,000 |

### Bidding
- **Highest Volume** almost always (volumes too low for reliable Cost Cap)
- If lead quality dips, add qualifying questions to the Instant Form

## KPI Targets

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 3 | Month 6 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Cost per booked job | Baseline | -15% | -25% |
| Lead→job rate | Track | Improve | Improve |
| Response time | <5 min | <5 min | <5 min |

## Common Pitfalls

- Radius too small → audience saturates, frequency spikes, CPMs climb
- Slow lead follow-up kills otherwise cheap leads
- No review/social proof in creative
- Not excluding past customers where the service is one-and-done
