# E-Commerce Meta Creative Playbook

> Meta industry template — used by the meta-plan skill for e-commerce campaign planning

## Overview

This playbook covers 5 core campaign types for e-commerce on Meta, each with
recommended creative assets, placement priorities, banana domain modes, and
budget guidance. Use alongside `copy-frameworks.md` for ad copy structure. Every
asset set should ship as *distinct concepts* to avoid Andromeda clustering.

## Product Launch

**Creative assets needed:**
- Hero product shot (clean/studio) for Feed
- Lifestyle image (product in real-world context)
- Carousel showcasing 3-5 key features
- Unboxing UGC video, 9:16 sound-on (15-30s) for Reels/Stories
- Comparison graphic (new vs. previous version)

**Placements (by priority):** Reels > Instagram/Facebook Feed > Stories
**Banana domain mode:** Product
**Aspect ratios:** 4:5 / 1:1 (Feed), 9:16 (Reels, Stories)
**Copy framework:** AIDA (cold audience, new-product awareness)
**Budget allocation:** 30-40% of the launch budget
**Key metrics:** Reach, CTR, add-to-cart rate, CPA

## Sale / Promotion

**Creative assets needed:**
- Bold sale creative with the discount clearly legible in-frame
- Before/after price comparison
- Urgency framing (ends Sunday / limited stock) in copy and creative
- Bundle-deal carousel (3-4 combos)
- Testimonial video highlighting savings

**Placements (by priority):** Feed > Reels > Stories > Advantage+ Shopping
**Banana domain mode:** Editorial
**Aspect ratios:** 4:5 / 1:1 (Feed), 9:16 (Reels, Stories)
**Copy framework:** PAS (agitate the FOMO, solve with the deal)
**Budget allocation:** 20-30% of the campaign budget
**Key metrics:** ROAS, conversion rate, AOV, revenue per click

## Seasonal

**Creative assets needed:**
- Themed hero image (holiday, back-to-school, summer)
- Gift-guide carousel (5-8 products)
- Seasonal lifestyle video (15s, sound-on)
- Limited-edition product spotlight
- Social-proof overlay (ratings, reviews, bestseller badge)

**Placements (by priority):** Reels > Feed > Stories > Advantage+ Shopping
**Banana domain mode:** Cinema
**Aspect ratios:** 4:5 / 1:1 (Feed), 9:16 (Reels, Stories)
**Copy framework:** BAB (before: seasonal struggle; after: solved with product)
**Budget allocation:** 15-25% of the campaign budget
**Key metrics:** Revenue, ROAS, new-customer rate, repeat-purchase rate

## Retargeting

**Creative assets needed:**
- Dynamic product ad (catalog/DPA, auto-populated)
- Cart-abandonment reminder with the product image
- Customer-review spotlight (star rating overlay)
- Low-stock / urgency graphic
- Cross-sell carousel (complementary products)

**Placements (by priority):** Feed > Stories > Reels > Audience Network
**Banana domain mode:** Product
**Aspect ratios:** 1:1 / 4:5 (Feed), 9:16 (Stories/Reels)
**Copy framework:** PAS (they left without buying; come back with an incentive)
**Budget allocation:** 10-15% of the campaign budget
**Key metrics:** ROAS, cost per conversion, cart-recovery rate, frequency

## Brand Awareness

**Creative assets needed:**
- Brand-story video (30-60s)
- Founder / behind-the-scenes footage
- Value-proposition creative
- UGC compilation reel (customer stories)
- Aspirational lifestyle imagery

**Placements (by priority):** Reels > Feed > Stories
**Banana domain mode:** Cinema
**Aspect ratios:** 9:16 (Reels, Stories), 4:5 / 1:1 (Feed)
**Copy framework:** Star-Story-Solution (brand as the hero narrative)
**Budget allocation:** 10-15% of the campaign budget
**Key metrics:** Video view rate, reach, brand lift, engagement rate, CPM

## Meta Creative Rules

- **Legible offer, not text-walls:** heavy text still delivers, but keep the key
  message legible and the frame uncluttered; front-load the value.
- **Primary text** truncates after ~125 characters on mobile — front-load the hook.
- **Reels outperform static** for awareness (often 2-3x reach); design 9:16 first.
- **Safe zones:** keep text/logos/CTAs out of the Reels/Stories top ~250px and
  bottom ~340px UI zones.
- **Sound-on** for Reels/Stories with captions; Feed is often sound-off, so caption everything.
- **Distinct concepts** beat variants — Andromeda clusters ads >60% similar.

## A/B Testing Matrix (Meta Experiments)

| Campaign Type    | Test Variable 1       | Test Variable 2      | Test Variable 3         |
|------------------|-----------------------|----------------------|-------------------------|
| Product Launch   | Hero shot vs. UGC     | AIDA vs. BAB copy    | Static vs. video        |
| Sale / Promotion | % vs. $ discount      | Urgency vs. none     | Single product vs. bundle |
| Seasonal         | Themed vs. neutral    | Gift guide vs. single | 15s vs. 30s video      |
| Retargeting      | Dynamic vs. static    | Incentive vs. none   | Review overlay vs. plain |
| Brand Awareness  | Founder vs. customer UGC | 30s vs. 60s       | Cinematic vs. raw       |

Run each test for a minimum of 7 days (and until each cell exits the learning
phase) before drawing conclusions. Isolate one variable per test, allocate
10-15% of budget to testing, then scale the winner with the remaining budget.
