# B2B Enterprise Meta Advertising Template

## Industry Characteristics

- Long, multi-stakeholder sales cycles; Meta's role is demand gen + retargeting, not last-click
- Success = pipeline (MQL→SQL→opportunity), measured well beyond the form fill
- High deal value justifies higher CPL, but lead quality is everything
- Meta complements (doesn't replace) sales-led and ABM motions
- Content offers (reports, webinars, ROI tools) outperform "book a demo" cold

## Recommended Objective & Placement Mix

| Layer | Objective | Budget % | Why |
|-------|-----------|----------|-----|
| Core | Leads (content offer / Instant Form) | 40-55% | Capture demand with a low-friction offer |
| Retargeting | Leads (demo) to warm accounts | 25-35% | Convert engaged accounts/contacts |
| Awareness | Video views (thought leadership) | 10-20% | Seed the accounts Meta will retarget |
| Testing | New offers / audiences | 5-10% | Find the message that resonates |

## Campaign Architecture

```
Ad Account
├── Awareness (Video views) — category education / POV
├── Prospecting (Leads) — content offer
│   ├── Ad Set: CRM/ABM Custom Audience (target accounts)
│   └── Ad Set: 1-3% Lookalike of customers / opportunities
├── Retargeting (Leads) — demo/consult for engaged contacts
└── Testing — offers, formats, hooks
```

## Creative Strategy

- **Thought-leadership / talking-head** video from a credible expert
- **Report / benchmark** lead magnets ("2026 State of X")
- **Case study** proof with a named logo and a hard metric
- **ROI / cost-of-inaction** angles for the economic buyer
- Professional but human — avoid stiff corporate stock; test UGC-style founder content

## Targeting Strategy

- **CRM / ABM Custom Audiences** (hashed target-account contact lists)
- **Lookalikes** of closed-won and of high-value opportunities
- **Retargeting** site + content engagers; suppress current customers
- Meta lacks job-title precision — lean on Custom Audiences + lead-form qualifiers rather than interest guesses

## Budget & Benchmarks

| Metric | B2B enterprise Meta benchmark (directional) |
|--------|---------------------------------------------|
| Meta CPM | $12-$28 |
| Cost per content lead | $20-$120 |
| Cost per demo/SQL | often $150-$600+ |
| Min viable monthly budget | ~$5,000+ for meaningful pipeline signal |

### Bidding
- **Highest Volume** for content-offer volume; the funnel qualifies quality downstream
- Import **MQL/SQL/opportunity** stages via CAPI so Meta optimizes to pipeline, not form fills

## KPI Targets

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 3 | Month 6 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Cost per SQL | Baseline | -15% | -25% |
| Lead→SQL rate | Track | Improve | Improve |
| Pipeline influenced | Track | Growing | Growing |

## Common Pitfalls

- Judging Meta on last-click demos instead of pipeline influence
- Optimizing to raw leads with no downstream stage imported
- "Book a demo" to cold audiences (offer a lower-friction content step first)
- One-and-done creative; enterprise buyers need repeated, varied touches
