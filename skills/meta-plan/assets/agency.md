# Agency Meta Advertising Template

## Industry Characteristics

- Managing **multiple client ad accounts** under one Business Manager
- Repeatable structure, naming, and reporting matter more than any single tactic
- Success = client retention via consistent CPA/ROAS and clear reporting
- Access, permissions, and asset ownership (pixels, pages, catalogs) are recurring pain points
- Each client is effectively one of the other industry templates underneath

## Recommended Operating Model

| Area | Standard |
|------|----------|
| Access | Client owns Business Manager + assets; agency gets partner access (never build on agency-owned pixels) |
| Structure | One repeatable campaign blueprint per client goal (Sales / Leads / App) |
| Naming | `META_[Client]_[Objective]_[Audience]_[Geo]_[Date]` |
| Reporting | Weekly scorecard: spend, CPA/ROAS, MER, creative diversity, learning-phase health |
| Creative | A distinct-concept pipeline per client (Andromeda-aware) |

## Campaign Architecture (per client)

```
Client Ad Account
├── Core conversion campaign (per client goal: Advantage+ Shopping / Sales / Leads)
├── Prospecting (Lookalike + broad)
├── Retargeting (site/engagement/customer list)
└── Testing (10% — new concepts, audiences)
```
Reuse the matching industry template (`ecommerce.md`, `saas.md`, `local-service.md`,
`b2b-enterprise.md`, etc.) for each client's specifics.

## Creative & Production

- Build a per-client **concept backlog** so no account starves for fresh, distinct creative
- Standardize placement exports (Feed 4:5/1:1, Reels/Stories 9:16) via the meta-generate skill
- Track fatigue and refresh cadence per client in the weekly scorecard

## Tracking & Governance

- Verify each client's **Pixel + CAPI + EMQ + dedup + domain verification** before scaling
- Confirm **catalog health** for e-commerce clients
- Keep a compliance checklist for regulated clients (Housing/Credit/Health Special Ad Categories)

## Budget & Benchmarks

- Benchmarks vary by client vertical — pull from the matching industry template
- Maintain a **portfolio view**: blended MER and per-client ROAS/CPA vs target
- Flag any client account breaching the 3x Kill Rule or with learning-limited ad sets

## KPI Targets (portfolio)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Client retention | High / churn-flagged early via scorecard |
| % accounts hitting CPA/ROAS target | Growing month over month |
| % ad sets learning-limited | <20% across the portfolio |
| Creative diversity per account | ≥10 distinct concepts live |

## Common Pitfalls

- Building on **agency-owned** pixels/pages (client can't take assets on offboarding)
- Copy-pasting one blueprint without adapting to the client's vertical template
- No standardized reporting → churn from perceived lack of value
- Letting a client account starve for fresh creative while others get attention
